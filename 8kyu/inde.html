My favorite comedian Conan O'Brien often gives this advice to those who are starting a new professional career: "Find your tribe!"

It's always harder alone. There will be doubts, there will be a lack of motivation, there will be moments of confusion when working with Figma and Webflow. But a good community can really pull you out of those moments, show you the way or simply give you a word of encouragement.

So I've created a student hangout on Discord. I didn't actually come up with this idea on my own. The students did. Many asked me for a place where they could connect and chat with other students. What a great idea.

Join the hangout here. (Try this alternative link if the first one doesn't work)

There you can chat directly with other students from the course, share content, exchange ideas, help each other with design, Figma, Webflow, and make friends.

While you are at it, join this Webflow Community too.

It's on Discord as well and they are incredibly helpful there.
